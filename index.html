13th Tribe

Innovative, inclusive and UNorthodox Judaism

Rabbi Sydney Mintz launched 13thTribe.org in 2021 to create collaborative Jewish projects and programs that inspire and expand Jewish life and spirit. 13th Tribe invites those who feel the calling, to experience a deeper and more creative Jewish engagement to join us. 13th Tribe exists to create and produce projects and partnerships including opportunities for deep learning, retreats, innovative media, sacred theatre, music and culture
